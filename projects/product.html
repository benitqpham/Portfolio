Product Renderings

Bosch Accelerometer (2025)

This project showcases a 3D visualization of a micromechanical structure inside a Bosch Accelerometer. The base geometry was carefully modeled to replicate the precise layout of the system, followed by simple texturing. To bring the model to life, the animation combines camera movements for dynamic presentation and a bending mechanic to demonstrate the structure's flexibility.
