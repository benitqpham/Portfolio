Photogrammetry

Old Hagia Sophia (2025)

An application is being developed for the Institute for Byzantine Archaeology in Freiburg to visualize different architectural components of the Hagia Sophia in Istanbul, Turkey. As part of the project, the individual elements will be digitally reconstructed using structure-from-motion (SfM) technology.
