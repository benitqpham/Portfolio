Motion Design

Digital Heaven - Social Hell (2025)

This project presents a series of commercial-style UX animations created in After Effects to reflect the evolution of digital releases over the past decade. By combining smooth motion design with familiar media imagery, it highlights advantages and benefits, but also downsides such as loneliness and the loss of in person communication.
